Who are we?

Perfecta Cosmetics and Skincare is a company that focuses on skincare. Our products are made with natural ingredients, hence our customers do not have to worry about toxic chemicals.

Mission Statement

Making skincare affordable and sustainable, which means having the least impact on the earth as possible.

Visions statement

Our goal is to become a leading figure in the cosmetology and skincare industry, proving to upcoming business that you can go green and still be successful. We aim to restructure the industry where beauty is aligned with nature and proper skincare is not limited to only those Who can afford expensive products.

Who is our target audience

We are targeting teens to young adults, who are starting to discover themselves. They will be doing research on what products to use to cure acne breakouts that tend to happen during puberty; getting them familiarized with natural, effective and sustainable alternative we aim to provide a healthier skincare journey.
